About Me

Hello, I’m Sara Strasner and I am a software developer and project manager. I have experience in JavaScript, CSS, and HTML and I am looking for work in the tech sector. I’ve worked individually and on teams to create a variety of web apps, ranging from escape room games to market research apps and everything in-between. All of my work has been created using git and gitHub, so I’m really comfortable using those tools. Not only do I have the technical skills of a developer, but I also bring 5 years of managerial experience overseeing people and projects from my previous role in Museum education. So I’m looking for a position where I can combine my soft skills with my technical knowledge.

Not only do I have the technical skills, but I am also passionate about technology. I originally got into museums because I want to make a positive difference in people’s lives, and I know that I can still have that impact in technology. I’m especially passionate about equity in access to technology and getting young people, especially women, interested in STEM careers.
